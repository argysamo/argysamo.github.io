About

Tate museum, London

Argyrios Samourkasidis is a PhD student with the Information Technology Group in Wageningen University. He holds a BSc in Electrical and Computer Engineering (5-year engineering curriculum) from Democritus University of Thrace since 2009 (top 10% of his class). His research interests are in the areas of Internet of Things, knowledge representation, environmental data interoperability, and web services. He is a Python developer with 5 years of experience. Argyris worked as a researcher in General Secretariat of Research and Technology (GSRT) and European Commission (FP7) funded projects, contributing in writing 8 deliverables in total. He holds a COBIT 5 Foundation and a Ecosystem Services Modeling certificate issued by ISACA and BC3, respectively. Argyrios has contributed six peer-reviewed publications.
