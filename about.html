About

Argyrios Samourkasidis is a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering department, Democritus University of Thrace (DUTH). He received his BSc in Electrical and Computer Engineering from DUTH in 2009 (top 10% of his class). His research interests are in the areas of knowledge representation, data and machine interoperability, Internet of Things, and web services. He is a senior Python developer with 4 years of experience, who worked as a researcher in General Secretariat of Research and Technology (GSRT) and European Commission (FP7) funded projects, contributing in writing 8 deliverables in total. He holds a COBIT 5 Foundation and a Ecosystem Services Modeling certificate issued by ISACA and BC3 , respectively. He has contributed 5 scientific publications.
